Connecting plant people

Here at Leaf Emporium our passion is two-fold: plants, and people. We have combined these two passions and brought together people and ideas from all around the world to bring our customers plant inspired decor and are working everyday to bring so much more, soon.

Handmade ceramics

These uniquely handmade ceramics come invarious plant inspired shapes, including: Monstera deliciosa, Fiddle-leaf fig, Strelitzia, Nasturtium, and Colocasia esculenta.

Wood leaves

These wooden leaves can be used in various creative and decorative excursions. They can be used for - but not limited to - pot holders, placemats, decorative platter, candle holder, and many more.

Grassroots at our core

All our products are sourced from within South Africa. We strive to build long lasting relationships with the people in our community - clients and suppliers alike.
